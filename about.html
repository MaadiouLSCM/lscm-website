About LSCM

From Planning Till Last Mile — serving the world's energy majors since 2006.

Who We Are

LSCM is a 5PL supply chain management company headquartered in Nouakchott, Mauritania, with operations spanning Nigeria, France, UAE, Senegal, Guinea, Ivory Coast, and beyond. We specialise in oil and gas logistics, EPC project cargo, customs clearance, and 3PL warehousing for the world's largest energy companies.

With over 20 years of industry experience, our team combines deep knowledge of West African trade corridors with the technology infrastructure of a modern logistics platform. Our proprietary CLEAR ERP system manages every shipment from Request for Collection through customs clearance to final delivery — with full ISO 9001 audit trail.

Our Journey

2006

Founded in Mauritania

LSCM established to serve the mining and energy sectors in Mauritania, initially supporting SNIM iron ore operations.

2010

Nigeria Operations Launch

Expanded to Lagos and Abuja to serve Shell SNEPCO, NAOC/Agip, and TotalEnergies upstream operations.

2014

West Africa Network

Opened offices in Senegal, Guinea, and Ivory Coast. Established multi-country customs clearance capability.

2018

France & UAE Hubs

European procurement hub in Le Havre and Middle East operations in Dubai to serve EPC supply chains.

2022

ISO 9001:2015 Certification

Bureau Veritas certified our quality management system. Digital transformation begins with CLEAR ERP development.

2025

CLEAR ERP v4 Launch

Proprietary freight forwarding platform goes live: 97 Prisma models, 486 endpoints, AI-powered automation, client portal.

2026

Takamul Energy Partnership

Major EPC mobilisation contract secured. CLEAR ERP enhanced with Rule Engine, POD signature capture, and predictive planning.
