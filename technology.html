Built for African
Energy Logistics

CLEAR is not an off-the-shelf product adapted for logistics. It was designed from the ground up for the specific challenges of freight forwarding across West Africa — multiple customs regimes, unreliable infrastructure, complex documentation requirements, and the demanding standards of energy majors.

  • Full offline resilience for field operations
  • Multi-currency with XOF, MRU, NGN, USD, EUR
  • Bilingual (English + French) across all surfaces
  • Real-time SSE event streaming to all connected clients
  • 50 automated cron jobs for compliance and monitoring
  • Complete audit trail for ISO 9001 certification
