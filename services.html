Our Services

End-to-end 5PL logistics for the oil & gas, mining, and energy sectors — from planning till last mile.

🛢️

Oil & Gas Logistics

Specialised freight forwarding for upstream and downstream oil & gas operations. From drill pipe to production modules, we handle cargo that others won't touch.

  • Offshore platform supply logistics
  • FPSO mobilisation support
  • Rig move coordination
  • Dangerous goods (IMDG/ADR) compliance
  • Tank container & ISO tank management
  • Oilfield equipment heavy-lift transport
🏗️

EPC Project Cargo

Full-scope project logistics for engineering, procurement, and construction contracts. We plan, route, and deliver oversized and heavy-lift cargo to remote sites.

  • Pre-feasibility transport studies
  • Route surveys & engineering assessments
  • Break-bulk & out-of-gauge cargo
  • Heavy-lift & crane coordination
  • Multi-modal transport planning (sea → road → barge)
  • Site delivery with last-mile coordination
🛃

Customs Clearance

Licensed customs brokerage across West Africa with deep knowledge of oil & gas duty exemptions, ECOWAS protocols, and fast-track clearance procedures.

  • Import/export declarations (Nigeria, Senegal, Mauritania, Ivory Coast)
  • HS code classification & tariff advisory
  • Temporary import permits (TIP)
  • Duty exemption management for oil & gas operators
  • Pre-arrival customs processing
  • Post-clearance audit support
🚢

Multimodal Freight

Sea, air, and road freight across 10+ countries. We optimise routes, consolidate shipments, and manage the full transport chain door-to-door.

  • FCL / LCL ocean freight (CMA CGM, MSC, Maersk)
  • Air freight (standard & charter)
  • Road haulage & cross-border trucking
  • Consolidation & groupage services
  • Reefer & temperature-controlled logistics
  • Intermodal transport optimisation
📦

3PL Warehousing & Distribution

Strategically located warehouses in Lagos, Nouakchott, and Dakar with inventory management, pick-and-pack, and FIFO-compliant storage for energy sector clients.

  • Bonded & free-zone warehousing
  • Inventory management with QR tracking
  • FIFO-compliant stock rotation
  • Pick, pack, and distribution
  • Cross-docking & transhipment
  • Hazmat storage (DG Class 1-9)
