// Frequently Asked Questions

What clients ask us
before we start.

What is a 5PL logistics provider? +

A 5PL (Fifth-Party Logistics) provider manages the entire supply chain on behalf of clients, from strategic planning and procurement coordination through to last-mile delivery. Unlike 3PL or 4PL providers, a 5PL like LSCM takes full ownership of supply chain design, technology integration, vendor management, and operational execution across multiple countries and transport modes.

Which oil and gas companies does LSCM work with? +

LSCM serves four major energy companies: Shell Nigeria (SNEPCO) for deep-water operations, NAOC (Nigerian Agip Oil Company), Agip as part of the ENI Group across West Africa, and TotalEnergies across multiple countries. We handle OCTG, heavy lift, subsea equipment, and time-critical rig supply chains.

In which countries does LSCM operate? +

LSCM operates in 10+ countries with local teams on the ground: Nigeria (headquarters and oil & gas hub), Senegal (West Africa operations), Ivory Coast (energy logistics), Mauritania (offshore & mining), Guinea (mining logistics), Dubai UAE (Middle East hub), France (Europe gateway), South Korea (EPC procurement), United Kingdom (global coordination), and the United States (Americas desk).

How does LSCM handle customs clearance in Nigeria? +

LSCM's customs clearance team operates daily across West African customs regimes with on-the-ground agents. Our services include temporary import bond management, duty optimisation, regulatory compliance for oil and gas equipment, and proactive customs hold prevention. Our proprietary CLEAR ERP system provides real-time tracking of customs status from Green Light through to final delivery.

What is LSCM's CLEAR ERP platform? +

CLEAR ERP is LSCM's proprietary supply chain management platform that provides real-time cargo tracking, automated KPI reporting, customs status monitoring, and full visibility from purchase order through customs clearance to final delivery confirmation. Clients can track every item via QR codes and receive automated status updates throughout the logistics chain.

How do I request a logistics quote? +

Submit a Request for Cargo (RFC) form on this page with your company details, origin, destination, transport mode preference, and cargo description. LSCM responds with a detailed quote within 48 hours. Alternatively, email contact@lscmltd.com or call +234 803 192 3528.
